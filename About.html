About me

Hello there, and thank you for checking out my website.

Although I have a background in mental health nursing specialising in health promotion, prevention and early intervention, I always felt somewhat constrained by what I perceived to be the limitations of the ‘medical’ model.

Whilst my clinical skills and experience continue to inform my on-going practice, working holistically and from a trauma informed, strengths based and solution focused approach was a far more natural fit.

I have a deep interest in self-growth and the field of wellbeing, and I believe wholeheartedly in our capacity for self-healing. We just need the ‘tools’ to support that process, and a commitment to take responsibility for our own health & wellbeing needs.

Even the most skilful, and resilient and resourceful amongst us however may experience periods of overwhelming distress, challenge, pain and crisis in our lives, and it is with this in mind, that I choose to help and support others to heal, to maximise their potential, and to once again find balance, vitality and fulfilment in their lives.
I feel very grateful to be working in this evolving field, and I look forward to connecting to you, and with you, soon

TRE (Tension & Trauma Releasing Exercises)

1:1 & Group Shake Sessions

“The magic of the TRE Process is that it can be utilised by anyone, any place on the planet. Even in cases of severe post-traumatic stress disorder, this process can re-establish a person’s equilibrium, stabilize their life, and return them to an emotionally and physically healthier place”.

David Berceli (Founder of TRE and CEO of Trauma Recovery Assessment & Prevention Services)

TRE is a body based self regulation tool. A simple set of physical preparation exercises are used to activate self induced therapeutic neurogenic tremors in the body. These tremors help to reset and rebalance the reflexes and habits in the central nervous.

In stress, tension and trauma, old primitive reflex parts of the brain may get stuck in defensive strategies. Over-protective reflexes contract our bodies for ‘fight and flight’ or shut down the body in ‘freeze’. TRE is a safe, natural and gentle way to wake up, connect to and truly ‘meet’ your.

What to wear: Please wear loose, comfortable clothing and non-slip socks if you’d rather not have bare feet.

Therapeutic Nurturing Touch Sessions

“Touch helps us feel real. Before language, our concepts are formed as we engage with a world of textures, resistance and other people”.

Steve Haines – ‘Touch is Really Strange’.

Touch therapy is one of the oldest forms of treatment in the world, having first been described in China during the second century BC and soon after in India and Egypt. Hippocrates, in 400 BC, defined medicine as ‘the art of rubbing’… The spontaneous and instinctive thing that we all do when we knock up against something hard and unyielding. Whether that ‘thing’ be a physical or emotional/psychological/spiritual hard boundary, the pain that we feel, the wound that we may sustain, lives on in us if we are not given the time space and tools to heal.

These therapeutic nurturing touch sessions are a gentle and respectful way to exercise personal agency in relation to getting your touch needs met on some deeper level.

What to expect: This fully clothed session shall commence with a seated hand/lower arm massage to promote the flow of oxytocin, followed by either a seated or lying down sound healing/tuning forks deep rest integration period.

TRANSITIONS Health & Wellbeing Sessions

“Wellbeing signifies that homeostasis is within the effective range”. Antonio Damasio (The Strange Order of Things – Life, Feeling, and the Making of Cultures)

A life transition is any change or adjustment that impacts your life in a significant way. We all experience transitions throughout our lives – big and small, planned and unplanned. Transitions may be centered around something exciting, such as starting a new job, moving house, or an addition to your family. They can also be prompted by distressing situations such as the death of a loved one or the breakdown of a relationship.

Whatever the circumstance, our capacity to tolerate the potential discomfort of the transition is determined by our default resilience and resourcefulness. Even the most self-regulated and skillful amongst us may require some assistance, guidance and support at times. These sessions are an opportunity to take stock of what is on all levels, to assess what aspects of your life require attention in both the short and longer term, and to then methodically work toward initiating the essential changes to bring more health, balance and vitality into your daily life..

What to expect: During the initial session you will complete a comprehensive health and wellbeing questionnaire, including a personal, professional and medical history. You shall then complete two very quick and simple colour therapy assessment tests.

Although a simple process, the tests themselves are a deep psychological tool that shall very accurately identify areas of holistic stress (physical, emotional, psychological and spiritual).

Based on this, we shall have a discussion about your concerns and those areas of existing and potential stress that require your mindful attention. We then move onto the ‘Human Givens in8 Skills’ tool for decision making, problem solving, strengthening relationships, reducing stress and improving well-being.

Utilizing a trauma informed, strengths based and solution focused approach, we shall explore and identify some life and health supporting goals to work towards restoring and improving your sense of wellbeing. By session end you shall have a clearer idea of those areas of your life that require your attention and committed input, and an action plan to support these potential changes.

Follow up sessions involve discussion of any actions taken since the previous session to initiate and support positive change. You shall then retake the colour tests and Human Givens in8 Skills tool, and set new intentions moving forward.
